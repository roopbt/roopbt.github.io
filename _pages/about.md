I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Bioscience and Biotechnology at the Indian Institute of Technology Kharagpur, India, under the supervision of Prof. Pinaki Sar. My research focuses on microbial ecology and ecosystem functioning, particularly investigating how abiotic stressors influence soil microbial communities and their ecological interactions.
